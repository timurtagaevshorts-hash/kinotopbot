Privacy Policy

Application: Verse

Developer: TULKINJON KULMATALIEV

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Verse is a social communication platform that allows users to share reels, communicate through chat, make voice and video calls, and access premium features. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the application, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • User ID
  • Messages exchanged through chat
  • Voice and video call information required to establish calls
  • Uploaded photos, videos and reels
  • Push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • Device information necessary for application functionality
  • Payment card number (Humo card) only when the user requests a payout.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide messaging and communication features.
  • Enable voice and video calls.
  • Store and display uploaded content.
  • Send notifications.
  • Calculate reel views and creator earnings.
  • Process payout requests submitted by users.
  • Improve security and prevent abuse.

4. User Earnings

Verse allows eligible creators to earn money based on reel views. When requesting a payout, users may voluntarily provide their Humo card number. The card number is used solely for processing the payout requested by the user.

5. Third-Party Services

The application uses third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Google Play Billing
  • Google Play Services

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect user information. Data transmitted between the application and our services is encrypted using secure connections.

7. Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data through the application or by contacting the developer using the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

Verse is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available on this page.
